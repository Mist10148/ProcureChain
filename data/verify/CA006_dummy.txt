PROCURECHAIN DUMMY DOCUMENT
Document ID: CA006
Title: School Tablet Procurement Batch 1
Category: Contract Agreement
Office: City Education Office
Agreement Date: 2026-02-14

Program Background:
The city seeks to support blended learning by procuring classroom tablet devices
for Grade 6 and Grade 10 students in pilot public schools.

Agreement Scope:
1. Supply of 1,200 education-grade tablet units
2. Device management software subscription, 12 months
3. Protective cases and charging carts
4. Teacher orientation and basic troubleshooting workshop

Financial Terms:
- Hardware package: PHP 10,800,000
- Software and device management: PHP 1,200,000
- Training and deployment support: PHP 350,000
- Total agreement value: PHP 12,350,000

Delivery Plan:
- Batch 1 (600 units): within 20 calendar days
- Batch 2 (600 units): within 40 calendar days
- Full deployment target: before start of Q3 class cycle

Minimum Technical Standards:
- Battery life minimum of 10 hours
- Device OS must support managed classroom mode
- Warranty coverage of at least 18 months

Data Protection and Use:
- Devices must implement user access controls
- Pre-installed apps limited to approved educational tools
- Student data collection must follow privacy rules

Beneficiary Impact:
The initial deployment targets 8 pilot schools and is expected to improve access
to digital modules, especially in sections with limited printed materials.
