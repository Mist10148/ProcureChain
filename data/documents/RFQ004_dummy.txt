PROCURECHAIN DUMMY DOCUMENT
Document ID: RFQ004
Title: Community Wi-Fi Expansion Lots 1-3
Category: RFQ
Issuing Unit: ICT Development Office
Release Date: 2026-01-28

Project Objective:
Procure managed internet and hotspot equipment services to expand free community
Wi-Fi access across 18 barangay centers.

Requested Quotations Include:
1. Outdoor access points with weather protection, 60 units
2. Core gateway appliances with bandwidth management, 6 units
3. Fiber backhaul setup and configuration services
4. 12-month managed maintenance and monitoring support

Technical Requirements:
- Minimum aggregate throughput of 1 Gbps per lot
- VLAN segmentation for public and admin traffic
- Built-in content filtering and abuse-prevention controls
- Uptime target of 99.5% monthly availability

Commercial Requirements:
- Breakdown of hardware, labor, and subscription costs
- Price validity for at least 60 calendar days
- Payment terms aligned with government procurement policies

Evaluation Criteria:
- 40% technical compliance
- 35% total evaluated cost
- 15% implementation timeline
- 10% after-sales support capability

Status Note:
This RFQ cycle was rejected due to non-compliant technical submissions in Lot 2
and incomplete proof of support coverage in two bidder proposals.

Follow-up Action:
Prepare revised RFQ package with clarified minimum specs and stricter document
checklist prior to re-posting.
