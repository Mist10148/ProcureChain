Category: Contract
Department: Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office
Contract Draft Date: 2026-02-03

3. Initial spare parts kit and emergency consumables
4. Operator and maintenance training for DRRM personnel

Duration and Milestones:
- Effective date: upon notice to proceed
- Delivery completion: within 35 calendar days
- System acceptance test: within 7 days after installation

Service Levels:
- Maximum response time for critical defects: 24 hours
- Preventive maintenance: monthly for first 6 months
- Warranty period: 24 months from acceptance

- Supplier must pass safety and commissioning inspections
- All equipment shall meet required capacity and certification standards
- Delays are subject to liquidated damages based on contract terms
