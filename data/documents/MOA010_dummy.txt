PROCURECHAIN DUMMY DOCUMENT
Document ID: MOA010
Title: Solar Streetlight Pilot
Category: Memorandum of Agreement
Lead Office: City Planning and Development Office
Draft Date: 2026-01-30

Parties to the Agreement:
- City Government
- GreenGrid Renewable Solutions Cooperative

Program Objective:
Pilot deployment of solar-powered streetlights in low-visibility pedestrian zones
to improve public safety and reduce power consumption.

Scope and Obligations:
City Government shall:
1. Provide approved installation sites and permits
2. Assign focal personnel for implementation monitoring
3. Coordinate barangay-level community orientation

Cooperative shall:
1. Supply and install 120 solar streetlight units
2. Provide maintenance support for 18 months
3. Submit quarterly performance and uptime reports

Pilot Budget Context:
- Equipment and installation estimate: PHP 6,900,000
- Monitoring and maintenance estimate: PHP 700,000
- Total pilot value reference: PHP 7,600,000

Key Milestones:
- Site validation: within 15 days of MOA signing
- Installation completion: within 60 days
- Performance review checkpoint: 6 months after activation

Review Outcome Note:
This MOA draft was rejected after legal and financial review due to unresolved
asset-ownership transfer clauses and incomplete warranty penalty provisions.

Recommended Revision Focus:
- Clarify ownership and turnover responsibilities
- Add measurable uptime obligations and non-performance penalties
