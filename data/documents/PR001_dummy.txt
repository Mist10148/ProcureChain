Document ID: PR001
Title: Office Supplies Q1 Framework
Category: Purchase Request
Department: General Services Office
Prepared By: Procurement Office
Date Prepared: 2026-01-12

Executive Summary:
This purchase request covers recurring office consumables for all city hall departments
for the first quarter. The request supports uninterrupted records processing, permit
printing, and public service desk operations.

Scope of Request:
1. A4 bond paper, 80 gsm, 5,000 reams
2. Black toner cartridges compatible with approved printer models, 320 units
3. Legal-size file folders, 15,000 pieces
4. Archiving labels and transparent sleeves for records management

Estimated Budget Breakdown:
- Bond paper: PHP 1,500,000
- Toner cartridges: PHP 960,000
- Filing materials: PHP 210,000
- Contingency and delivery: PHP 80,000
- Total requested amount: PHP 2,750,000

Operational Justification:
Current stock levels are forecasted to last only until week 2 of February based on
average consumption from the previous fiscal year. Delayed procurement may result in
queue backlogs for business permits, civil registry releases, and treasury reports.

Delivery and Timeline:
- Purchase order target issuance: 2026-02-01
- Delivery window: 15 calendar days after PO release
- Inspection and acceptance: within 3 working days after full delivery

Compliance Requirements:
- All items must comply with government-approved quality standards
- Bidder must submit product data sheets and sample units for validation
- Supplier must provide replacement policy for defective items

Risk Notes:
- Risk: delayed delivery due to supply shortages
	Mitigation: include liquidated damages clause and staged delivery fallback
- Risk: incompatible consumables
	Mitigation: require compatibility matrix during technical evaluation

Acceptance Criteria:
The request is considered fulfilled when all listed quantities are delivered,
quality-checked, and accepted by end-user departments with signed receiving reports.
