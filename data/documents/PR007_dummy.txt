Document ID: PR007
Title: Urban Greening Tools and Seedlings
Category: Purchase Request
Department: Environment and Natural Resources Office
Prepared Date: 2026-01-22

Request Purpose:
Support the city urban greening campaign by providing tools and plant materials
for barangay-led tree planting and roadside beautification.

Requested Items:
1. Tree seedlings (native species), 8,000 units
2. Ornamental shrubs, 5,500 units
3. Digging tools set, 220 kits
4. Organic fertilizer packs, 4,000 bags

Cost Estimate:
- Seedlings and shrubs: PHP 1,450,000
- Tools and logistics: PHP 620,000
- Fertilizer and planting support materials: PHP 310,000
- Total requested budget: PHP 2,380,000

Implementation Notes:
- Planting will prioritize flood-prone and heat-vulnerable corridors
- Barangay volunteers will be assigned per cluster for maintenance
- Survival monitoring will be conducted every 45 days

Risk Considerations:
- Risk: low survival rate during dry weeks
	Mitigation: include watering schedule and replacement stock
- Risk: delayed delivery of seedlings
	Mitigation: split orders among accredited nurseries

Status Note:
Awaiting final approver decision before purchase order issuance.
